SECTION 6: NOVELTY & INVENTIVE STEP STATEMENT

Novelty:

The present invention provides a novel combination of: (1) Real-time multi-platform creator data aggregation and normalization, (2) Dynamic persona adaptation mechanisms, (3) Proprietary multi-dimensional affinity scoring algorithms, (4) Integrated workflow state machine for deal management, (5) Comprehensive value assessment framework for intangible creator assets.

Inventive Step/Non-Obviousness:

The invention is non-obvious to a person having ordinary skill in the art because: (1) The technical problem of platform fragmentation in creator commerce was not previously recognized as requiring unified solution, (2) Dynamic persona adaptation while maintaining creator authenticity is a non-obvious technical challenge, (3) The multi-dimensional affinity scoring incorporating audience quality metrics represents novel combination.

SECTION 7: INDUSTRIAL APPLICABILITY

The invention is industrially applicable as it:

  • Addresses real commercial need in multi-billion dollar creator commerce industry
  • Can be implemented using standard software engineering practices
  • Supports scalable architecture for millions of creators and thousands of brands
  • Provides multiple revenue models (commissions, premium features, API access)
  • Meets active market demand from brands and creators
