FAQ (QUICK ANSWERS):
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Do I need a patent lawyer?
A: No for filing. You can file yourself to save ₹50-100K. Get lawyer later
   if examiner raises objections.

Q: Is ₹1,600 the only cost?
A: For provisional filing, yes. Full application (in 12 months) costs ₹9,000.
   Total: ~₹10,600 for basic protection.

Q: What if I miss the 12-month deadline?
A: Application is abandoned and you lose priority date. Mark calendar now!

Q: Can I still sell my product while filing?
A: Yes! Patent pending status gives you legal rights during prosecution.

Q: How long until patent is granted?
A: Typically 18-36 months from filing. You have legal protection after
   filing (patent pending).

Q: What if someone steals my idea before the patent grants?
A: You can still sue them after the patent grants for damages during
   pending period.

Q: Can I extend the patent beyond 20 years?
A: No in most jurisdictions. But you can create new patents on improvements.

Q: What about filing internationally?
A: Consider PCT filing within 12 months (costs $2-5K) for protection in
   150+ countries.
