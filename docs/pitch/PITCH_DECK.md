## Slide 3: Solution

### Verifiable Reputation That Travels With You

**Valueskins creates a portable, on-chain reputation system:**

1. **Connect** your social accounts
2. **Verify** your metrics (followers, engagement, history)
3. **Build** a score based on real performance
4. **Own** your reputation as an NFT
5. **Transact** with brands who trust your verified level

*Think: Credit score for creators, but transparent and portable.*

## Slide 5: How It Works

```
Creator connects accounts → Algorithm scores activity → 
Score stored on-chain → Brands query API → 
Match made → Escrowed payment → Deal completed → Score updates
```

**Key Innovation:** The scoring algorithm is published and auditable. 
No black boxes. Creators can simulate their own score.
