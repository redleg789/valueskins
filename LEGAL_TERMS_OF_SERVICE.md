- Provide evidence: messages, deliverables, payment records, screenshots

### 9.2 Investigation Timeline
- **48 hours:** Initial review and acknowledgment
- **5 business days:** Full investigation and evidence gathering
- **7 business days:** Decision issued

### 9.3 Decision Outcomes
- **Awarded to Creator:** Brand must pay full amount + chargeback fees ($50)
- **Awarded to Brand:** Creator must return payment or provide deliverables; platform commission refunded
- **Partial Resolution:** Split payment (e.g., 50/50) if both parties partially at fault
- **No Clear Evidence:** Dispute dismissed; payment stands as-is

### 9.4 Appeals
- Either party may appeal within **7 days** of decision with new evidence
- Appeals are reviewed by a different team member
- Appeal decision is final and binding

### 9.5 Escalation
- If either party disagrees with final decision, dispute may be escalated to:
