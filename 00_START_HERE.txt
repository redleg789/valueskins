================================================================================
                    ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Can I edit the PDFs?
A: Yes! They are fully editable. You can:
   - Add your details to forms
   - Change applicant information
   - Print and sign manually
   - Re-save as PDF after editing

Q: What if I make a mistake?
A: You can:
   - Edit the PDF file again
   - Print, sign, and re-upload
   - Contact Patent Office if needed
   - The forms are flexible until submitted

Q: Why 4 documents?
A: Indian Patent Office requires:
   1. Main specification (patent details)
   2. Form 1A (examination request)
   3. Form 3 (inventor declaration)
   4. Drawings/Figures (technical diagrams)

Q: Why are the claims so broad?
A: Strategy - to maximize coverage while staying defensible:
   - Claims 1-2 are intentionally broad to catch most implementations
   - Claims 3-10 add specificity to support the broad claims
   - This makes it hard for examiners to reject AND hard for competitors
     to design workarounds

Q: What if my application gets rejected?
A: Normal process:
   - Most applications get at least one office action (objection)
   - You can respond with arguments or claim amendments
   - Budget ₹10,000-30,000 for potential attorney help
   - The process typically takes 24-36 months

Q: Can I file internationally?
A: Yes! After provisional filing:
   - File PCT application within 12 months (gives 150+ countries)
   - Cost: $2,000-5,000 for PCT
   - Do this before filing full Indian application if going global

Q: What does "patent pending" mean?
A: After filing:
   - You can use "Patent Pending" notice
   - Application number gives legal protection
   - Publication happens in 18 months
   - Full protection once patent grants
