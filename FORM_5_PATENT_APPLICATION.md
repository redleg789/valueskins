### Abstract (Summary - 150 words max):

A system and method for facilitating value exchange between digital content creators and brand entities through intelligent matching and workflow management. The system comprises: (1) real-time multi-platform creator data ingestion, (2) dynamic creator profile synthesis, (3) brand-creator affinity matching engine, (4) multi-stage workflow state machine, (5) multi-platform commerce integration, and (6) comprehensive value assessment framework. The system solves platform fragmentation, information asymmetry, workflow complexity, and creator value quantification through a unified, intelligent interface that adapts creator presentation to brand requirements while maintaining authenticity and ensuring compliance.
