## 1. Business Model

### 1.1 Revenue Model

ValueSkins earns a **5% commission on every deal**:

```
Deal Value: $1,000
```

- Low enough to be attractive vs. Fiverr (20-25%) or Upwork (10-20%)

## 2. Unit Economics

### 2.1 Per-Deal Economics

```
Average Deal Value: $2,500 (conservative estimate)
Commission Rate: 5%
Revenue per Deal: $2,500 × 5% = $125

Variable Costs per Deal:
  - Payment processing fee (Stripe): 2.9% + $0.30 = $72.80
  - Payout processing (ACH): $0.25
  - Support time (~5 min @ $50/hr): $4.17
  - Infrastructure/hosting (prorated): $1.00
  - Total Variable Cost: ~$78.22

Gross Profit per Deal: $125 - $78.22 = $46.78
Gross Margin: 46.78 / 125 = 37.4%
```

### 2.2 Creator Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Assumption:** All acquisition is organic (no paid marketing) for MVP.

```
CAC = $0 (organic)
Time to LTV: Immediate (no spend to acquire)
```

### 2.3 Creator Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Assumptions:**
- Average creator does 2 deals/month
- Average deal value: $2,500
- Creator stays active for 24 months (conservative)
- Commission: 5% = $125/deal

```
Monthly Revenue per Creator: 2 deals × $2,500 × 5% = $250
Gross Margin: 37.4%
Monthly Gross Profit per Creator: $250 × 37.4% = $93.50

LTV (24 months): $93.50 × 24 = $2,244

Payback Period: Immediate (no CAC, so payback = 0 days)
LTV:CAC Ratio: Infinite (no spend)
```

## 6. Break-Even Analysis

### 6.1 When Does ValueSkins Break Even?

**Fixed Monthly Costs:**
```
Salaries: $3K (support, when hired)
Infrastructure: $2.6K
Tools: $0.2K
Legal/Misc: $0.3K
Total Fixed: $6.1K/month
```

**Variable Costs per Deal:**
- Stripe fee: 2.9% of $2,500 = $72.50
- Support time: $4

**Commission per Deal:** $125

**Contribution Margin:** $125 - ($72.50 + $4) = $48.50 per deal

**Break-even Deals per Month:**
```
$6,100 / $48.50 = 126 deals/month
```

**Timeline:**
```
Month 1: ~3 deals → NOT breakeven
Month 2: ~20 deals → NOT breakeven
Month 3: ~100 deals → ALMOST breakeven
Month 4: ~200 deals → BREAKEVEN + $3.2K profit
Month 5+: Profitable
```

**Result:** ValueSkins breaks even in **Month 4** (if projections hold)
